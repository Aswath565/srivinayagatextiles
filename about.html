Our Story

Sri Vinayaga Textiles is a leading yarn and fabric supplier and distributor based in Tirupur, the textile hub of India. Founded by Mr. Sakthivel V, we bring over two decades of expertise in the yarn and fabric industry, specializing in high-quality cotton, polyester, and viscose yarns for B2B buyers and textile businesses worldwide.

  • Our Network in Tiruppur

    As one of the most trusted yarn and fabric suppliers in Tiruppur, we maintain strong relationships with leading textile mills across Tamil Nadu. Our extensive network allows us to source and supply premium quality yarns including ring spun, open end, and melange yarns to meet the diverse needs of the textile industry.

  • Yarn Supply Expertise

    Specializing in both natural and synthetic yarns, we offer a comprehensive range of products including 100% cotton yarn, polyester cotton blends, and viscose yarns. Our expertise in yarn supply ensures consistent quality, superior strength, and excellent dyeability for all your textile requirements.

  • B2B Yarn Solutions

    As a leading B2B yarn supplier in Tiruppur, we are committed to providing reliable, high-quality yarn products with competitive pricing and on-time delivery. Our dedicated team works closely with clients to understand their specific requirements and deliver customized textile solutions that drive their business success.
