Premium Woven Fabrics

Our woven fabric collection features a diverse range of designs and textures, crafted with precision to meet your unique application requirements. Sourced from high-quality yarns and produced using advanced weaving techniques, these fabrics ensure durability, elegance, and superior finish.

  • Yarn dyed fabrics for vibrant colors and patterns
  • Striped fabrics for a classic and timeless look
  • Checkered designs for a unique and stylish appearance
  • Seersucker fabric for a textured and breathable finish
  • Jacquard woven for intricate designs and patterns

Product Specifications

  • Appearance: Various designs and textures
  • Material: High-quality yarns
  • Weight: 200-400 gsm
  • Width: 150-200 cm
  • Shrinkage: ≤ 3%
  • Color Fastness: ≥ 4

Applications

Our woven fabrics are versatile and can be used in various applications across the garment and home textile industry:

  • Garment manufacturing for men's and women's wear
  • Home textile products such as bedding, curtains, and upholstery
  • Industrial applications such as filtration and medical textiles

Health Benefits

Our woven fabrics are made from natural fibers and are breathable, moisture-wicking, and hypoallergenic:

  • Regulates body temperature for comfort and relaxation
  • Prevents moisture buildup and reduces the risk of skin irritation
  • Soft and gentle on the skin, reducing the risk of allergic reactions

  • Bale packing for bulk orders
  • Custom packaging as per client needs
