Our Hosiery Yarn Collection

We offer a wide range of premium hosiery fabrics catering to garment exporters and apparel brands. Our fabrics are made from high-quality yarns to ensure durability, softness, and excellent dyeing capability for varied apparel applications.

Each batch undergoes rigorous quality control measures, including testing for moisture content, particle size, and active compounds. Our state-of-the-art processing facility ensures that the fabrics retain maximum quality while meeting international textile standards.

Available Fabric Types

  • Single Jersey Fabric: Ideal for T-shirts, innerwear, and casual wear garments.
  • Loop Knit Fabric (Unbrushed): Used for lightweight sweatshirts and casual tops.
  • Fleece Fabric (Brushed): Soft and warm fabric suitable for hoodies and winter wear.
  • Velour Fabric: Plush, smooth fabric widely used for premium tracksuits and loungewear.
  • Patony Fabric: Known for its unique texture and quality finish for designer garments.
  • All Types of Jacquard Fabric: Intricate designs knitted into fabric for stylish apparel.
  • Auto Striped Fabric: Fashionable striped fabric for T-shirts and sportswear.
  • Plated Fabric: Fabric with dual colour effects used in modern casual wear.
  • Other Special Designed Fabrics: Custom knit patterns based on client requirements.

Applications

These fabrics are widely used in:

  • Casual wear supply
  • Sportswear and gym wear
  • Winter wear garments
  • Fashionable designer collections
  • Kidswear and innerwear segments

Packaging & Supply

Fabrics are supplied in roll forms with standard packaging or customized packing as per buyer requirement. MOQ, pricing, and lead time vary depending on fabric type and order volume.
